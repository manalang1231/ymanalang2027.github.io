HISTORY AND FOUNDING FATHERS


Altaïr Ibn-La' Ahad

Sherlock Holmes


The 2 great founders of Altair Academy. Altaïr Ibn-La’ Ahad is a Master Assassin and mentor during the Levantine Brotherhood of the Assassins. Though he was devoted to their Assassin’s Creed, he wanted to also create a place of learning for the young where they could not only learn about the skills of an assassin and warrior, but also the knowledge that can be attained through the Pieces of Eden. That is why he went to the world's greatest detective and a close friend, Sherlock Holmes and created what we call Altair Academy today. Detective Sherlock Holmes agreed to co-found the academy with Altair as he also believed in his mission of creating a safe heaven for fighting but he especially wanted the newer generations to learn of the different sciences and to learn what he has learned throughout his lifetime. The detective knew of the vast knowledge the Piece of Eden held and he knew if they could share even a fraction of the knowledge to their students, the world would greatly benefit from it. That is who our great founders are.

Altair Academy

A High School with one of the best curriculums in the world. It is a sanctuary for the scientific spirit, technological advancements, analysis of literature, the curiosities of art, and the discipline of combat. As Altair in Arabic means “Flying eagle” We believe that our students are flying eagles and will soar high through the skies of success.


Our Kurriculum:

Science and Technology:

We teach science and all of its methods, branches, and we give the students the choice to take these different scientific branches as their majors. We also teach technology like robotics and engineering which works hand in hand with science.

Literature:

We like to teach them the importance of being able to understand different literary pieces. We believe that to be well rounded you need to be able to read between the lines and be able to comprehend different forms of speech.

Arts:

Arts is something that everyone has inside of them and we like to expose our students to different kinds of art like painting, sculpture, sketching, theater, film, and other forms of art. Art allows the students to think creatively and creativity is a very important trait.

Kombat:

We take pride in our specialization of combat. We think that everyone should be able to defend themselves and we teach different types of combat skills. We teach fencing, sword fighting, axe throwing, parkour, judo, muay thai, kendo, iaido, arnis, kenjutsu, archery, basic gun safety practices, sometimes allow students to learn gunsmithing and our favorite, Mortal Kombat.
